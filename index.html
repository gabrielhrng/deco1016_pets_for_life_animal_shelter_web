100% VOLUNTEER RUN

Pets for Life is a 100% volunteer led animal shelter, as we receive no goverment funding. Funds for food and vet care come from our fundraisers and donations.

NON EUTHANISING

Our shelter is a non euthanising adoption centre, meaning we aim to be pro-life wherever possible when maintaining our cats' welfare.

OUR RESPONSIBILTY

For every cat that is admitted to our shelter, vaccinations, micro-chipping and desexing procedures are done before they are considered to be re-homed.
