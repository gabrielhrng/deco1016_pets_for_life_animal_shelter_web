About Us

Pets For Life has been rescuing, caring for and re-homing lost, abandoned, surrendered and mistreated cats since 2000. With no government funding, we depend entirely on the funds we raise from donations, fundraising and adoptions to provide the finances to provide food and vet care for our cats.

Our Mission

At Pets for Life Animal Shelter Inc, we aim to:

  • Provide short or long term care to abandoned, surrendered, mistreated or lost cats;
  • Provide veterinary care, vaccinations, micro-chipping and desexing procedures to all cats in its care;
  • Rehabilitate all cats in its care and re-home wherever possible;
  • Be a non euthanising organisation wherever possible.
