Make A Difference!

Since our shelter is 100% Volunteer run, we need people with loving hearts, willing to help our furry friends! Tasks include:

  • • Following the shelter roster

    • This includes cage cleaning, litter box maintenance, feeding, general shelter cleaning, laundry, grooming and medicating (when necessary) and of course, socialising the cats and kittens.

  • • Administration
  • • Fostering
  • • Fundraising
  • • Shelter maintenance
  • • Pet placement
  • • Provision co-ordination


Volunteering can be a very rewarding experience! We hope to have you to join our volunteers team!


Requirements For Volunteers

  • All volunteers should have a current tetanus vaccine.
  • All volunteers must be 16 years of age or older. Volunteers between the ages of 13 and 16 must be accompanied by a parent or legal guardian.
  • Shelter work is physical – bending and lifting is extensive. If you can’t handle physical work, consider joining one of our committees, such as fundraising.
  • Volunteers must enjoy direct contact with cats and have some experience in handling them.
  • Average time needed is 2-4 hours weekly or fortnightly or work around your schedule.
  • Volunteers are needed mornings, late afternoons, weekdays and weekends.
  • Volunteers must provide their own transportation to the shelter in Billinudgel.
