Every Donation Makes a Difference!

Pets for Life Animal Shelter Inc is a NSW registered charity run totally by volunteers. Our team is dedicated to the welfare of the cats and kittens that come to us at the shelter. We are a non euthanising adoption centre, and annually re-home over 100 cats and kittens in the Byron Shire.

Pets for Life has DGR status, therefore all donations over $2 and tax deductable.

What Can I Donate To?

  • Supply Me For a Month!

    $40.00 will feed one of our cats or kittens for a month.

  • Help Me Through An Operation

    $125.00 will help us pay for a vet to desex a cat or kitten at our shelter.

  • Donate Specified Amount

    Any donation will be used it to help cover the costs of running the shelter and caring for our cats and kittens.

What We Do

Pets For Life has been rescuing, caring for and re-homing lost, abandoned, surrendered and mistreated cats since 2000. We receive no government funding and depend entirely on the funds we raise from donations, fundraising and adoptions to provide the finances to provide food and vet care for our cats.
